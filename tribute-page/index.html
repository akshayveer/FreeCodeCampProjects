Early Life

He was born on February 24, 1995 by two college students in Wisconsin who put him up for adpation. Steve Jobs was adopted by Clara & Paul Jobs as infant. Once he had adopted he lived in California.

Search for Career

Steve Jobs searched for a carrer he wanted to pursue, unfortunately he lacked direction. He enrolled at Reed College but six months later he dropped out. Later he attended creative classes and then went from a video designer to finding spiritual guidance. Just before he invented Apple he experienced Psychdelic drugs.

Friend and Business Partner

When Steve Jobs was attending Homestead High School, he was introduced with Steve Wozniak. In 1976, when Steve Jobs was only 21, him and Wozniak started Apple Computers. The two started their entrepreneur business by jobs selling his Volkswagen & Wozniak selling his scientific calculator, using Jobs family garage as workshop.

Innovations

Apple II

The Apple II is an 8-bit home computer, one of the first highly successful mass-produced microcomputer products and was the first consumer product sold by Apple Computer.

Apple Lisa

The Lisa is a personal computer designed by Apple Computer, Inc. during the early 1980s. It was the first personal computer to offer a graphical user interface in a machine aimed at individual business users

Macintosh

Jobs introduced the original Macintosh computer on January 24, 1984. This was the first mass-market personal computer featuring an integral graphical user interface and mouse.

NeXT Computers

After Jobs was forced out of Apple, he started a company that built workstation computers. The NeXT Computer was the basis for today's macOS and iOS

iMac

Apple iMac was introduced in 1998 and its innovative design was directly the result of Jobs's return to Apple. The iMac also featured some technical innovations, such as having USB ports as the only device inputs

iTunes

iTunes is a media player, media library, online radio broadcaster, and mobile device management application developed by Apple Inc. Through the iTunes Store, users can purchase and download music, music videos, television shows, audiobooks, podcasts, movies, and movie rentals in some countries

iPod

The major innovation of the iPod was its small size achieved.The introduction of the iPod resulted in Apple becoming a major player in the music industry

iPhone

First iPhone was released on June 29, 2007. Time Magazine declared it "Invention of the Year". The Apple iPhone is a small device with multimedia capabilities and functions as a quad-band touch screen smartphone.Siri, a virtual assistant capable of voice recognition is introduec with iPhone 4S.

iPad

iPad is an iOS-based line of tablet computers designed and marketed by Apple Inc. The user interface is built around the device's multi-touch screen, including a virtual keyboard. The iPad includes built-in Wi-Fi and cellular connectivity on select models.

Health Issues

In 2003 Jobs had found out that he had a neuroendocrine tumour, a form of pancreatic cancer, rare but operable. He then had a surgery in 2004. In 2009 Jobs had lost weight he then had a liver transplant. Later he died in October 5,2011
